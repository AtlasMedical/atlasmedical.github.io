Team

Daniel Zhou

Daniel is one of two electrical designers at Atlas Medical. He leverages his extensive experience in printed circuit board design and fabrication while working on the electronics to interface to the sensor.

Jake Fisher

Jake is both the project manager and lead firmware developer for Atlas Medical. Jake tracks the team's deliverables, coordinates team resources, and develops the embedded software that runs on the sensing and pneumatics microcontrollers.

Colin Cooke

Colin is the lead software developer for Atlas Medical, but he is also responsible for pneumatics design and fabrication. Colin develops the analysis software used to extract insights from the full body sensor matrix.

Michael Jonas

Michael is one of two electrical designers at Atlas Medical. Michael works primarily on sensor design, but he also assists with the development of Atlas Medical's custom printed circuit boards.
